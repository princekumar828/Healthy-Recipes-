Indian Breakfast Recipes

Poha

Ingredients:

  • Flattened rice (poha)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Onions
  • Peas
  • Peanuts
  • Turmeric powder
  • Coriander leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse flattened rice and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, peas, and peanuts. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  4. Add turmeric powder and rinsed flattened rice. Mix well.
  5. Cook until the poha is heated through. Garnish with coriander leaves and lemon juice.
  6. Serve hot.

Upma

Ingredients:

  • Semolina (suji/rava)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal
  • Chana dal
  • Curry leaves
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast semolina in a pan until it turns golden brown. Set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, and chana dal.
  3. Add curry leaves, chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and cook until they are slightly tender.
  5. Add roasted semolina and water. Mix well to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until the upma is thickened. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • Dosa batter
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal
  • Curry leaves
  • Turmeric powder
  • Oil
  • Ghee
  • Chutney and sambar for serving

Instructions:

  1. Boil and mash potatoes. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and turmeric powder. Sauté until onions are golden.
  4. Add mashed potatoes and mix well. Set aside the masala filling.
  5. Heat a dosa pan, pour dosa batter, and spread it thinly.
  6. Place a portion of the masala filling in the center of the dosa. Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Idli with Coconut Chutney

Ingredients:

  • Idli batter
  • Coconut
  • Green chilies
  • Urad dal
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Steam idlis using idli batter and set aside.
  2. For coconut chutney, blend coconut, green chilies, and salt into a smooth paste.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves. Pour this tempering into the chutney.
  4. Serve idlis with coconut chutney.

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander leaves
  • Ghee
  • Yogurt for serving
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. For the dough, knead whole wheat flour with water and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  2. Boil, peel, and mash potatoes. Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, cumin seeds, and coriander leaves to the mashed potatoes.
  3. Divide the dough and potato mixture into small balls.
  4. Roll out the dough ball, place a portion of the potato mixture, and seal the edges to form a stuffed paratha.
  5. Cook on a griddle with ghee until both sides are golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with yogurt.
