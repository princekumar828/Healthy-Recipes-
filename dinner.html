Indian Dinner Recipes

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Cashews
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Butter
  • Fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in butter until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, cashews, and spices, cook until tomatoes are soft.
  3. Blend into a smooth paste.
  4. Sauté paneer cubes in a little butter.
  5. Add the blended paste and fresh cream, simmer until well combined.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add chicken pieces and simmer until cooked through.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.

Vegetarian Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, biryani masala)
  • Ghee
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in ghee until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until partially tender.
  4. Layer with parboiled rice, saffron-infused milk, and fresh mint and coriander leaves.
  5. Cook until rice is done and serve.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients:

  • Yellow lentils (dal)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook yellow lentils until soft.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in ghee until golden.
  3. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and spices.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add cooked lentils and simmer until flavors meld.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.

Roti with Baingan Bharta

Ingredients:

  • Eggplants (baingan)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Roti (Indian flatbread)
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast eggplants until the skin is charred.
  2. Peel and mash the roasted eggplants.
  3. Sauté onions, green chilies, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  5. Add mashed eggplants and simmer until well combined.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with roti.
