Indian Lunch Recipes

Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer until cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and serve.

Chana Masala

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer until flavors meld.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, biryani masala)
  • Ghee
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in ghee until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until partially tender.
  4. Layer with parboiled rice and cook until rice is done.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint and coriander leaves and serve.

Rajma (Kidney Bean Curry)

Ingredients:

  • Kidney beans
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook soaked kidney beans until tender.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add cooked kidney beans and simmer until flavors meld.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve.

Methi Thepla

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Fresh fenugreek leaves (methi)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander)
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine whole wheat flour, chopped fresh fenugreek leaves, yogurt, and spices.
  2. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest.
  3. Roll out small discs and cook on a griddle with a little oil.
  4. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
